Dr. Narang is formerly a physician at Advocate Health Care specializing in Cardiology, Advanced Heart Failure and Transplant Cardiology, and Internal Medicine. He earned his MD at University of Illinois at Chicago College of Medicine and completed his residency at University of Chicago Medical Center (UCM). Later, he completed fellowships in Cardiovascular Disease and Advanced Heart Failure & Transplantation at UCM.
